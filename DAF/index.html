OUR PROGRAMS

Explore unlimited possibilities

dschool Africa Foundation was established with the aim of exposing Youth and Women to the world of science, technology, engineering and mathematics careers..

Early Learning Projects

dSchool Africa Foundation created tutorials for both learners and teachers with the objective of ensuring that every African school can teach coding from the foundation phase in their way of understanding.

STEM Projects for Women

The Project is meant to increase interest in STEM Education through a practical way as they develop positive attitude. This helps students to gain self-confidence related to the STEM subjects which in turn increases the employment opportunities.
Our goal is to establish mentorship programs to provide guidance , support and inspiration to girls interested in STEM fields

Raincoat Projects

This project aims to protect unprivileged school going children from pneumonia and health issues during the rainy season.
We target are children from public primary schools, day high schools and street children to be protected from rains during the heavy rainy season .
The distribution is done to those who don’t have adequate protection and safety from rains and the diseases that are associated with it
